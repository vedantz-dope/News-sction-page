VIT Bhopal University

HEALTH-O-TECH CLUB

PULSE 1.O

The Health-O-Tech club recently conducted a remarkable two-day offline event on the 28th and 29th of July 2023, titled PULSE 1.0. The event left an indelible mark on all attendees with the esteemed presence of the Chief Guest, Mr.Varun Singla, popularly known for his YouTube educational channel Gate Smashers and a massive subscriber count of 1.5 million as the Chief guest. The event was initiated by the traditional lighting of the lamp, followed by a heartwarming welcome speech. Mr.Singla delivered captivating career guidance speech interwoven with personal anecdotes from his college life that resonated deeply with the audience.
